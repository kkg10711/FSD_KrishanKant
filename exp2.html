Famous Footballers

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentinian footballer who has won multiple Ballon d'Or awards. He played for Barcelona, PSG, and now Inter Miami.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese footballer known for his incredible goal-scoring ability. He has played for Sporting, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al-Nassr.

Neymar Jr

Neymar Jr is a Brazilian footballer known for his dribbling skills. He has played for Santos, Barcelona, PSG, and Al-Hilal.
